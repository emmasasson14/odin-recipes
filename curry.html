Thai Red Curry Recipe

Back to Home

Description

Thai curry (Thai: แกง, pronounced [kɛ̄ːŋ]) is a dish in Thai cuisine made from curry paste, coconut milk or water, meat, seafood, vegetables or fruit, and herbs. Curries in Thailand mainly differ from the curries in Indian cuisine in their use of ingredients such as herbs and aromatic leaves over a mix of spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Jasmine White Rice
  • 1 can (14 ounces) regular Coconut Milk
  • 2 Tablespoons of Thai Red Curry paste
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 Tablespoon of Coconut Oil
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper chopped
  • 1/2 cup of Snap Peas
  • *** optional 1/4 cup of chopped Basil
  • *** optional s1/3 cup cubed firm tofu

Steps

  1. To cook the rice, if using a rice maker, place 1 cup of rice and two cups of water in the maker. Cook until done. If using a stove, fill the pot with 2 cups of water and set to boil. Place the rice into the pot and lower the water to simmer. Cook for 30 minutes or until all the water is gone. Fluff with fork and (** Optional: Add a Tablespoon of coconut oil).
  2. In a saucepan, heat up the coconut oil on medium heat. Add the garlic and stir until golden.
  3. Next, add the chopped red bell pepper, and snap peas, stir on medium heat for about 4 minutes or until tender.
  4. Add the can of coconut milk and thai curry paste, and continue to stir. Turn down the heat and let simmer for 10 minutes, stir occasionally.
  5. *** Optional: Add tofu and basil
  6. Serve over rice and add fresh basil on top as a garnish
